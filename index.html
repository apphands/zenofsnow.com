A beautiful and peaceful snow globe world. Shake to "stir up" the snow and then watch it slowly fall. We call this Zen of Snow because it is so peaceful to watch. Featuring animated lights and scenery.

For the iPhone/iPad.

Features:

  1. 4 snow scenes (animated Christmas lights twinkle, snow falls, creek flows, smoke rises from cabin's chimney)

  2. Shake to stir up snow and watch it fall.

  3. Christmas music "Angels We Have Heard on High", "Silent Night", "Midnight Clear"

  4. Swipe to pan between the four scenes.
